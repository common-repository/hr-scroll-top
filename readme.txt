== üìì About HR Scroll Top ==

**HR Scroll Top** is a very useful Scroll To Top plugin. HR Scroll Top plugin has more icons so that you can choose any icons on your website. You can change the icon's background color and icon color. Icon moving option is available on also left or right side. Here has the smooth scroll speed option. You will be capable of speed increase or decrease to your need.
Everybody can use this plugin on their websites. If you want to go up smoothly on your website, you can use this plugin. It is best to scroll to the top of a website page. "HR Scroll Top" is easy to use.
